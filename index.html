About

By the acheivers, for the aspirants.

Freeacademy is a self-study portal for various examinations held across India. Where study materals & live session of respective rank holders is available at ZERO Cost. It is a free platform where every aspiring student is invited to have indepth knowledge along with tips & techniques to crack these examinations. The team of faculty members are equipped with great knowledge acquired from being a student & faculty members in various prestigious institues like IIT, NIT, AIIMS, IIM, IIIT, GFIT, etc & some members from different government sector (IAS, IES, IPS, IFS, IRS, SDM, SDO, SE/AE/AEEE in PSUs, SM, Engineer, Doctor, Manager, etc.

Advantages
  1. We charge only your prestigious time for making you availabe with such informative content.
  2. Most of the facuty members have already cracked either one or various examination as stated above, which assures your success in the examination
  3. We have very user friendy modules.
  4. You can comeback to us with your queries, doubts & suggestion to make our content better.
We are also in growing process as you are, let us support each other to bring the best out of us. Your time is the most important & valuable fee which you have to pay us. Keep Learning :) Wish you a very Happy Learning.
Vision
Our vision is to encourage FREE EDUCATION for aspirants who cannot afford costly study materals of various prestigious coaching instituions. Our intention is that each & every aspirants gets equal opportunites to fulfill his/her dream without financial hurdles. We beleive that our country will progress only when our youth is educated.
Motive
Our Motive is to provide best study material for various examinations on a single platform without any cost to the aspirant who are prepring for the respective examinations. We also want tot provide live sessions from person who’ve cracked the examinations to provide proper guidance for the challenges faced during preparation & methods to tackle those problems & bring motivation for the aspirants.
